# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Self-directed retail investors who manage their own portfolios and want institutional-quality research without a hedge fund budget. They open AgentFloor in the morning — often before or during market hours — to check holdings, read the AI briefing, and decide whether any position needs deeper analysis or a scheduled watchlist run.

## Product Purpose

AgentFloor is a self-hosted AI investment research command center. It tracks portfolios (CSV upload or manual entry), generates weekday morning briefings with health scores and action items, runs multi-agent deep analysis on any ticker, and monitors watchlists on a schedule. Research and education only — no trade execution, no financial advice. Success means the user feels informed enough to make their own decisions with confidence, backed by transparent AI reasoning and outcome tracking over time.

## Brand Personality

Sharp, modern, accessible. Consumer fintech clarity (Robinhood / Wealthfront lane) applied to a serious research tool — not a toy, not a terminal. Voice is direct and helpful: explain financial impact, not just model jargon. Professional without being cold; confident without hype.

## Anti-references

- Legacy enterprise admin panels — gray boxes, cramped tables, tiny type, 2005-era density
- Gamified trading apps — streaks, confetti, casino energy, urgency theater
- Generic AI slop — purple gradient chrome, chatbot-first layouts, vague "AI magic" copy
- Crypto meme aesthetics — neon gradients, rocket emojis, hype-driven CTAs

## Design Principles

1. **Morning-first clarity** — The daily portfolio check is the hero workflow. Surface what changed, what needs attention, and what can wait.
2. **Confidence through transparency** — Show reasoning behind verdicts (analyst reports, regime signals, debate). Never hide the "why" behind a badge.
3. **Research over spectacle** — Data density is fine when scannable; decoration is not. Every visual element should help interpret holdings, signals, or risk.
4. **Consumer polish, pro substance** — Simplified navigation and readable typography like modern fintech; depth available on drill-down, not on every screen.
5. **Trust by default** — Self-hosted, research-only positioning should feel calm and responsible. Disclaimers are visible, not buried.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Pragmatic baseline: fix obvious contrast, keyboard, and focus issues; respect `prefers-reduced-motion`; keep interactive targets usable. No formal WCAG certification target, but avoid known failures (muted gray body text on tinted backgrounds, clipped dropdowns, icon-only controls without labels).
